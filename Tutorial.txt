_ MOUSE
	Chuot trai (mouse_left) = Next background
	Chuot phai (mouse_right) = Next song
	con lãn = tam dung (pause or resume) music

_ KEY (Ban Phim)
	Up = Next song
	Down = Pre song (TurnBack)
	Left = Pre Background (turnback)
	Right = Next Background 
	
	Space = Setup Firework (cac che do khac nhau)
	
	e = Effect
	b = balloon firework
